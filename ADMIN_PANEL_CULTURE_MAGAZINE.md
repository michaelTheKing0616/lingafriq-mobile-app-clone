# Culture Magazine Management in Admin Panel

## Access the Admin Panel

**URL:** https://lingafriq-admin.web.app/

**Login Credentials:**
- Use your admin account credentials

## Culture Magazine Feature

The Culture Magazine management feature allows you to:
- ✅ View all articles (published and unpublished)
- ✅ Review scraped articles
- ✅ Edit article content, categories, and metadata
- ✅ Publish/unpublish articles
- ✅ Feature articles
- ✅ Delete articles
- ✅ Filter by category, country, published status
- ✅ Search articles

## How to Review and Publish Articles

### Step 1: Navigate to Culture Magazine

1. Log in to https://lingafriq-admin.web.app/
2. Look for **"Culture Magazine"** or **"Articles"** in the sidebar menu
3. Click to open the Culture Magazine management page

### Step 2: View Unpublished Articles

1. In the Culture Magazine page, you'll see a table of all articles
2. Use the **filter** dropdown to select **"Published: No"** or **"Status: Unpublished"**
3. Click **"Apply Filter"** to see only unpublished articles
4. Articles are sorted by creation date (newest first)

### Step 3: Review an Article

1. Click on an article title or the **"View"** button
2. Review the article details:
   - **Title** - Check for accuracy
   - **Content** - Review for quality and appropriateness
   - **Category** - Verify it's correct
   - **Country** - Check accuracy
   - **Source URL** - Verify it works
   - **Tags** - Check relevance
   - **Excerpt** - Review summary

### Step 4: Edit Article (if needed)

1. Click the **"Edit"** button (pencil icon) on the article
2. Make changes to any field:
   - Fix typos in title or content
   - Update category if wrong
   - Correct country information
   - Add or remove tags
   - Update excerpt
3. Click **"Save"** when done

### Step 5: Publish Article

**Option A: Publish from List View**
1. Find the article in the list
2. Click the **checkbox** next to the article
3. Click **"Bulk Actions"** dropdown
4. Select **"Publish"** or **"Update Status"**
5. Set **Published** to **Yes**
6. Click **"Apply"**

**Option B: Publish from Edit View**
1. Open the article for editing
2. Find the **"Published"** checkbox or toggle
3. Check the box or toggle to **"Yes"**
4. Optionally set **"Published Date"** to today
5. Click **"Save"**

### Step 6: Feature an Article

1. Open the article for editing
2. Find the **"Featured"** checkbox or toggle
3. Check the box to feature the article
4. Featured articles appear prominently in the mobile app
5. Click **"Save"**

### Step 7: Bulk Publish Multiple Articles

1. Select multiple articles using checkboxes
2. Click **"Bulk Actions"** → **"Publish"**
3. Confirm the action
4. All selected articles will be published

### Step 8: Delete Articles

1. Select article(s) using checkbox
2. Click **"Bulk Actions"** → **"Delete"**
3. Confirm deletion
4. **Warning:** This permanently deletes the article!

## Filtering and Search

### Filter by Status
- **All** - Show all articles
- **Published** - Only published articles
- **Unpublished** - Only unpublished articles (for review)

### Filter by Category
- Tradition
- Cuisine
- Music
- History
- Language
- Festivals
- Art
- Literature

### Filter by Country
- Filter articles by specific African countries

### Search
- Use the search bar to find articles by title, content, or tags

## Workflow Recommendations

### Daily Workflow
1. **Morning:** Check for new unpublished articles (from daily scraper)
2. **Review:** Read through 5-10 articles for quality
3. **Edit:** Fix any issues (typos, categories, etc.)
4. **Publish:** Publish quality articles
5. **Feature:** Feature the best 2-3 articles

### Weekly Workflow
1. Review all unpublished articles
2. Bulk publish articles that meet quality standards
3. Feature top articles for the week
4. Check analytics for popular articles

## Tips

- **Quality over Quantity:** Only publish well-written, accurate articles
- **Check Sources:** Verify source URLs are valid
- **Categorize Correctly:** Ensure articles are in the right category
- **Feature Wisely:** Only feature high-quality, engaging articles
- **Regular Reviews:** Set aside time daily/weekly for reviews
